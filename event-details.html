Homecoming Reunion 2026

Classes of 2016, 2018 & 2020 return to Amphitheatre for live music, way too much catching up, and the Dean's annual karaoke attempt.

Sat, Oct 17, 2026 · 5:00 PM – 11:00 PM
Amphitheatre, Adtu Campus
212 alumni going

About this event

Three graduating classes, one stage, and a night that always somehow runs two hours longer than planned. Expect live music from The Campus Collective (three of the five members are Adtu alumni themselves), a food truck line-up curated by the Class of 2018 hospitality crowd, and the Dean's now-infamous karaoke hour. Bring a valid ID for entry — and low expectations for the karaoke.
